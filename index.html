Hyaluronic Acid

+

What It Is

A naturally occurring molecule in our skin that can hold up to 1000 times its weight in water. Our hyaluronic acid is plant-derived and biofermented for maximum efficacy.

Benefits

  • Supports skin barrier function
  • Suitable for all skin types

Frequently Asked Questions

Are your products cruelty-free? ▼

Yes, absolutely. All Lumière products are 100% cruelty-free and vegan. We never test on animals and ensure that our suppliers follow the same ethical standards. We are certified by Leaping Bunny and PETA.

What is your return policy? ▼

We offer a 30-day satisfaction guarantee. If you're not completely happy with your purchase, you can return it for a full refund within 30 days. Products should be at least 50% full to qualify for returns. We also offer free exchanges on all orders.

How long does shipping take? ▼

Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days. We also offer express shipping (2-3 business days) and next-day delivery in select areas. All orders over $75 qualify for free standard shipping. You'll receive tracking information once your order ships.

Are your products suitable for sensitive skin? ▼

Most of our products are formulated for sensitive skin and are free from common irritants like parabens, sulfates, synthetic fragrances, and harsh chemicals. However, we recommend checking the ingredient list for each product and doing a patch test before use, especially if you have known allergies.

Do you offer samples? ▼

Yes! We offer a sample set featuring mini versions of our bestselling products. You can also add up to 3 free samples to any order at checkout. We believe you should try before committing to full-size products.

How should I store my products? ▼

Store products in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Most products have a 12-month shelf life after opening. Products with vitamin C should be kept refrigerated to maintain potency. Always close lids tightly after use to prevent oxidation.
